My Love for Skiing

Utah and Colorado mountains are my happy place

Why I Ski

Skiing is one of my favorite hobbies. There is nothing better than fresh powder and a clear blue sky in the mountains of Utah or Colorado.

My Top Resorts

  1. Park City, Utah
    • Biggest resort in the US
    • Close to Salt Lake City
  2. Snowbird, Utah
    • Amazing powder days
    • Very challenging terrain
  3. Vail, Colorado
    • Huge back bowls
    • Great for a ski trip
  4. Breckenridge, Colorado
    • Highest chairlift in North America
    • Fun town to explore
